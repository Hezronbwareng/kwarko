Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to apply for a home loan?

Typically, you'll need documents such as proof of income, tax returns, bank statements, and identification.

What is the difference between a fixed-rate and adjustable-rate mortgage?

A fixed-rate mortgage has a set interest rate for the entire loan term, while an adjustable-rate mortgage (ARM) has a rate that can change periodically.

Can I apply for a home loan with a low credit score?

While a higher credit score improves your chances, some lenders offer options for borrowers with low credit scores.
